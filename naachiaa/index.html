About Us

Naachiaa Estates Ltd

Naachiaa Estates limited, a subsidiary of Naachiaa Group of companies was established in the year 1999. Naachiaa Estates Ltd is basically a concrete product manufacturing company. In Ghana, the best concrete products in the country can all be found in Naachiaa Estates Ltd.
At NEL, all our operations have been mechanized to ensure the maximum efficiency of our well trained personnel. Currently, the company has 100 expertises to handle all aspect of our productions. The quality of our products has never been questioned, that is why we are still the best in the concrete manufacturing industry.
We believe in customer satisfaction. For this reason, NEL has an attractive delivery after sales package for anyone who purchases our products.
